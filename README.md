# E-commerce-sales-analysis-dashboard
Based on the data from E-commerce sales, using a variety of visuals, significant insights were extracted. Sales, profit, quantity, shipping cost and discount are identified as KPI's in the E-commerce sales data. Some visuals used are bar chart, donut chart, area chart, pie chart and tree map. 
* Sales by segment, category and market are visualised to provide insight to stakeholders.
* Filters are used to extract the information from desired category, market and shipping mode. 
* Visualized top 5 and bottom 5 sales by country and state respectively.
